अखिल भारतीय मुवेंदर मुन्ननि कळगम साचा:Infobox Indian Political PartyAll India Moovendar Munnani Kazhagam is a Tamil political party in India, based amongst the thevar caste. The party founder and president is Dr. N. Sethuraman. The party was founded by All India Thevar Peravai 1998.
All India Moovendar Munnani Kazhagam are allied with Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam. In the तमिळ नाडु legislative assembly elections of 2001 one candidate of All India Moovendar Munnani Kazhagam was launched on a DMK symbol in the constituency of Thirumangalam and got 39 918 votes .
All India Moovendar Munnani Kazhagam were opponents of the J. Jayalalithaa All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam government. For example they have protested against press censorship and abuse of anti terror laws under the AIADMK rule.
In 2006 elections the party contested in 5 constituencies with alliance with BJP and lost in all the 5 seats without deposit.The Party is now in alliance with All India Anna Dravidar Munnetra Kazhagam from August 2006.
The party also fights for the right to animal sacrifice.
